How we protect and use your information

VIRTUS LEONIS 3D PRIVACY POLICY

At Virtus Leonis 3D, we value and respect your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when using our software services, subscriptions (including Patreon), and technology development platforms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect these types of information based on your interaction with us:

  • Registration data: Name, email, Patreon username (if applicable), country, payment method (processed by third parties like Stripe/PayPal).
  • Technical data: IP address, hardware, operating system, usage of our programs (version, runtime, crashes).
  • Project content: 3D files, technical specifications, communications (for development clients).
  • Analytics: Cookies, interaction data with our website and software.

2. Use of Information

We use your data to:

  • Manage your access to licensed software or subscriptions (including Patreon benefits).
  • Provide technical support and updates.
  • Improve our products through usage analysis.
  • Communicate news (only with explicit consent).
  • Comply with legal and billing obligations.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Payment platforms: Patreon, Stripe or PayPal (only data necessary for transactions).
  • Technical providers: Hosting services, analytics (Google Analytics) and support.
  • Authorities: When required by law.
  • Development partners: Only for specific projects (under NDA).

4. Data Security

We implement:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for transfers.
  • Restricted access to sensitive data.
  • Regular security reviews.
  • Special protection for project files.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access, correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for communications.
  • Download your data in portable format.
  • Manage cookies through your browser.

Contact to exercise rights: virtusleonis3@gmail.com

6. Cookies and Technologies

We use:

  • Essential cookies: For login and basic functionality.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (you can disable them).
  • Local Storage: To save preferences in our programs.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes via email or through alerts in our software. The updated version will take effect immediately upon publication.
