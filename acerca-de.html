OUR STORY

Virtus Leonis 3D was born on March 8, 2025, from the vision of a group of software development enthusiasts seeking to revolutionize the way businesses create and use digital solutions. From our humble beginnings in a small studio in Boulder, Colorado, we have become a benchmark in software development in the United States.

Our name, which means "The Courage of the Lion" in Latin, reflects our bold and innovative approach to every project. We believe that the courage to explore new ideas is what has allowed us to stand out in an increasingly competitive market.

Today, with a team of more than 20 software development professionals, we have completed more than 150 projects for clients in 15 countries, always maintaining our commitment to quality and technological innovation.
